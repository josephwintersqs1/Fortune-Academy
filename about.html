WHO WE ARE

EMPOWERING PROFESSIONALS THROUGH QUALITY EDUCATION

Fortune Academy is a leading provider of professional training and continuing education across multiple industries. We specialize in delivering state-approved, industry-recognized courses that help individuals launch and advance their careers in Real Estate, Mortgage, Appraisal, OSHA Safety, Healthcare, and HR Compliance.

Our mission is to make quality professional education accessible, affordable, and flexible—meeting the diverse needs of working professionals and career changers through online, live virtual, and in-person learning options.

OUR JOURNEY

PIONEERS OF REAL ESTATE EDUCATION SINCE 1982

The story behind Fortune Academy is quite unlike any other. From being a one-man show in a motel room to having 11 locations across South Carolina, Fortune Academy has gone from strength to strength.

1982

The Beginning

Started as an entrepreneurial company in a motel room with a vision to educate real estate professionals better.

1990s

Building Partnerships

Formed strategic partnerships with Real associations such as CCAR and PeeDee Realtors.

2000s

Expansion & Innovation

Became the only school to offer multiple training delivery methods across 11 SC locations.

Today

150,000+ Professionals Trained
