Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Fortune Academy is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all individuals, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all users.

Standards and Guidelines

Fortune Academy strives to conform to Level AA of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

Accessibility Features

Our website and learning platform incorporate various features to enhance accessibility, including:

Navigation and Structure

  • Clear and consistent navigation throughout the site
  • Descriptive page titles and headings
  • Logical content structure and reading order
  • Skip navigation links to bypass repetitive content
  • Breadcrumb navigation for easy orientation

Visual Design

  • Sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • Information not conveyed by color alone
  • Resizable text that maintains readability
  • Responsive design that works on various screen sizes
  • Clear focus indicators for keyboard navigation

Content

  • Alternative text descriptions for images
  • Captions and transcripts for video content
  • Clear and simple language
  • Descriptive link text
  • Form labels and instructions
  • Error identification and suggestions

Keyboard Accessibility

  • All functionality accessible via keyboard
  • Logical tab order through page elements
  • No keyboard traps
  • Visible focus indicators

Screen Reader Compatibility

  • Semantic HTML markup for proper structure
  • ARIA labels and landmarks where appropriate
  • Descriptive page titles and headings
  • Text alternatives for non-text content

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with various assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Alternative input devices
  • Browser accessibility features

Course Accessibility

We strive to make our educational content accessible to all learners:

Video Content

  • Closed captions for all video lectures
  • Transcripts available for download
  • Audio descriptions where appropriate
  • Player controls accessible via keyboard

Course Materials

  • Downloadable PDF versions of course content
  • Accessible document formats
  • Alternative formats available upon request
  • Clear structure and formatting

Assessments and Exams

  • Extended time accommodations available
  • Screen reader compatible exam formats
  • Clear instructions and error messages
  • Multiple attempt opportunities

Accommodations and Support

Fortune Academy provides reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with disabilities. If you require specific accommodations to access our courses or complete your training, please contact us as early as possible. Accommodations may include:

  • Extended time for exams and assignments
  • Alternative format materials
  • Assistive technology support
  • Modified course schedules
  • Additional technical support

Third-Party Content

While we strive to ensure the accessibility of our own content, some third-party content embedded on our site (such as videos, documents, or tools from external providers) may not fully meet accessibility standards. We work with our vendors to improve accessibility and provide alternative formats when possible.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We regularly:

  • Conduct accessibility audits of our website and learning platform
  • Test with assistive technologies and diverse users
  • Train our staff on accessibility best practices
  • Update content to meet current standards
  • Incorporate accessibility into our design and development process
  • Review and update our accessibility policies

Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Fortune Academy. If you encounter accessibility barriers or need assistance:

Report an Accessibility Issue

Please let us know if you experience difficulty accessing any content or functionality. Provide as much detail as possible, including:

  • The specific page or content you had trouble accessing
  • A description of the problem
  • The assistive technology you were using
  • Your operating system and browser

Request Accommodations

If you need specific accommodations to access our courses, please contact us at least two weeks before you need the accommodation. We will work with you to find appropriate solutions.

Formal Complaint Process

If you have concerns about our accessibility practices that have not been resolved through our standard process, you may file a formal complaint. We take all complaints seriously and will investigate and respond within 10 business days.

Legal Standards

Fortune Academy complies with applicable federal and state accessibility laws, including:

  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • State-specific accessibility requirements
