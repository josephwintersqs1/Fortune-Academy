About This Program

This comprehensive 24-week program prepares you for dual certification as both a Surgical Technician (TS-C) and Sterile Processing Technician (CRCST). Study at your own pace with self-directed online modules, interactive simulations, and expert video instruction. Perfect for working adults or those who need flexibility while pursuing a rewarding healthcare career.

Program Curriculum

Module 1 Weeks 1-2

Professionalism in Allied Health

Introduction to healthcare professionalism, ethics, communication skills, and patient care fundamentals.

Module 2 Weeks 3-8

Human Anatomy & Medical Terminology

Comprehensive study of human body systems, anatomical structures, and essential medical terminology.

Module 3 Weeks 9-14

Sterile Processing Fundamentals

Decontamination, cleaning, disinfection, sterilization techniques, and instrument processing procedures.

Module 4 Weeks 15-24

Surgical Technology Training

Surgical procedures, operating room protocols, sterile techniques, and patient care during surgery.

Important Clinical Requirements

You must secure a clinical site before enrolling in this program. Upon completion of coursework and passing the certification exams, you will need to complete hands-on clinical requirements:

  • TS-C Certification: 125 surgical cases (30 general surgery + 75 in specialized areas) completed within 2 years of passing the exam
  • CRCST Certification: 400 hours of hands-on experience in a Central Services department within 6 months of passing the exam

Fortune Academy provides clinical placement assistance but cannot guarantee placement. You are responsible for securing your clinical site.
