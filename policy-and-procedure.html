Policy & Procedure

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Company Policies

Fortune Academy is committed to providing high-quality professional education and maintaining the highest standards of integrity, fairness, and transparency. These policies govern the operation of our educational programs and the relationship between Fortune Academy, our students, and corporate clients.

1.1 Code of Conduct

All students, instructors, and staff are expected to maintain professional conduct at all times. This includes:

  • Treating all individuals with respect, dignity, and courtesy
  • Maintaining honesty and integrity in all academic work
  • Respecting intellectual property rights and avoiding plagiarism
  • Complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and institutional policies
  • Refraining from any form of harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior

1.2 Non-Discrimination Policy

Fortune Academy does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic. We are committed to providing equal opportunities in education and employment.

2. Enrollment Policies

2.1 Course Registration

Students may register for courses online through our website or by contacting our enrollment team. Registration is considered complete upon receipt of payment and confirmation email. Course access is typically granted within 24 hours of enrollment.

2.2 Prerequisites

Some courses may have prerequisite requirements. Students are responsible for ensuring they meet all prerequisites before enrolling. Fortune Academy reserves the right to verify qualifications and deny enrollment if prerequisites are not met.

2.3 Age Requirements

Students must be at least 18 years of age to enroll in Fortune Academy courses, unless otherwise specified for a particular program. Proof of age may be required upon enrollment.

3. Course Policies

3.1 Course Access and Completion

Online courses provide access for a specified period (typically 6-12 months) from the date of enrollment. Students must complete all coursework, including exams, within the access period. Extensions may be available for an additional fee.

3.2 Academic Integrity

Students must complete all coursework independently unless group work is specifically authorized. Cheating, plagiarism, or any form of academic dishonesty may result in course failure, loss of certification, and dismissal from Fortune Academy without refund.

3.3 Examinations

Most courses include one or more examinations that must be passed to receive course completion credit. Students typically have multiple exam attempts at no additional cost. Specific passing scores and attempt limits are outlined in individual course descriptions.

3.4 Certificates of Completion

Upon successful completion of a course, students will receive a certificate of completion that can be downloaded immediately. Certificates include course details, completion date, and credit hours for submission to licensing boards.

4. Attendance and Participation

4.1 Live Virtual Classes

Students enrolled in live virtual classes must attend all scheduled sessions. Missing more than one session may result in course failure. Students who cannot attend must notify the instructor in advance.

4.2 In-Person Classes

Attendance is mandatory for all in-person class sessions. Students must arrive on time and participate actively. Excessive absences or tardiness may result in course failure.

5. Payment and Refund Policies

5.1 Payment Terms

Payment is due in full at the time of enrollment unless a payment plan has been approved. We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and purchase orders from approved organizations.

5.2 Refund Policy

Students may request a full refund within 7 days of enrollment if they have completed less than 25% of the course. After 7 days or after completing 25% of the course, no refunds will be issued. Refund requests must be submitted in writing to support@fortuneacademy.com.

5.3 Course Transfers

Students may transfer to a different course of equal or lesser value within 30 days of enrollment if they have completed less than 25% of the original course. Transfer fees may apply.

6. Technical Requirements

6.1 System Requirements

Students are responsible for ensuring they have the necessary technology and internet connectivity to access online courses. Minimum requirements include a computer or mobile device with internet access and a modern web browser.

6.2 Technical Support

Fortune Academy provides technical support during business hours (Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM EST). Students experiencing technical difficulties should contact support@fortuneacademy.com or call (800) 922-2245.

7. Student Records and Privacy

7.1 Record Retention

Fortune Academy maintains student records in accordance with state and federal requirements. Records include enrollment information, course completion data, transcripts, and certificates.

7.2 Transcript Requests

Students may request official transcripts at any time. There may be a fee for transcript processing and delivery.

8. Complaints and Grievances

8.1 Complaint Process

Students who have concerns or complaints should first contact the instructor or course coordinator. If the issue is not resolved, students may submit a formal complaint to support@fortuneacademy.com. All complaints will be investigated promptly and addressed according to our complaint resolution procedures.

9. Policy Changes

Fortune Academy reserves the right to modify these policies at any time. Students will be notified of significant policy changes via email. Continued enrollment after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the new policies.
